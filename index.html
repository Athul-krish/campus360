Departmen Info

Computer Science and Engineering

The CSE department focuses on software systems, algorithms, data structures, machine learning, and computer architecture. Students learn to design, develop, and optimize applications for real-world computing challenges.

Master of Computer Application

The MCA program prepares students for advanced application development, databases, enterprise systems, and IT consultancy. It emphasizes practical programming, system integration, and problem-solving skills.

Master of Business Administration

The MBA department develops leadership, strategic planning, finance, marketing, and operations management expertise. Students learn to manage organizations, lead teams, and drive business growth.

Electronics and Electrical engineering

The EEE department covers power systems, circuits, electronics, control systems, and renewable energy. It trains students in designing and maintaining reliable electrical and electronic systems.

Bachelor of Computer Application

The BCA program introduces core IT subjects such as programming, web development, databases, and software engineering. It builds a strong foundation for careers in computing and technology.

Integrated Master of Computer Application

The IMCA program integrates undergraduate and postgraduate studies in computer application, offering advanced training in programming, systems analysis, and research-oriented development over an extended term.
